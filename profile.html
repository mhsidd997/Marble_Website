About Company

It took nature millions of years to create natural stones. We at ABC will continue to bring them from different parts of the world to enhance the aesthetic appeal of your spaces. Uncompromising on quality, ABC will continue to deliver one of Mother Nature’s greatest gifts to mankind.

The People

ABC is a renowned brand in the natural stone industry, established two decades ago. Since this period, the people behind ABC have worked relentlessly to create a symbol of quality, and customer satisfaction. Through relationships with the best quarries around the globe, ABC has acquired the ability to source premium materials. ABC found its niche in the competitive Middle East market by proving time and again to its faithful clients that its product was and continues to be superior because of the right sourcing and pricing. At ABC, we consider marble to be precious, its beauty to be marveled at for generations to come, hence disallowing us from compromising the quality of our material from all angles and perspectives.

The Product

ABC specializes in premium imported Natural Stones handpicked from best quarries around the world, in countries such as Italy, Spain, Greece, Portugal, Brazil, Turkey, Macedonia, India, Pakistan, Egypt, China and many more. ABC is perfect for fulfilling your dreams of creating an extraordinary interior & exterior, through its vast treasury of natural stones of exotic & rare designs. Over the period of time ABC has added all possible colors of natural stones from every corner of the world to its stock. Today ABC stocks more than 250 colors of marble, granite, onyx, travertine, lime stone, sandstone & slate, so as to provide an extensive range of natural stones under one roof to it’s valued clientele.

The Performance

Since its inception, ABC, with its past experiences, vast knowledge of business, hard work, dedication to service and sincere efforts, is recognized as the largest stockist of Natural Stones in this part of the world.

The Policy

Ever since its foundation, it has been policy at ABC to stock quality materials with specification to befit the market here in the Middle East, using exact 20mm or 30mm thickness of slabs, homogeneity in thickness, controlled variation in color etc. in order to allow our customers to complete their projects successfully, in a timely manner through approval from their architects & consultants.

Today ABC appreciates its renowned reputation of excellence, owing to its competitive pricing and high quality. In addition, we believe that our relationships with major global giants of the Stone Industry, allow us to overcome all obstacles and ensure that our policies are kept sacred.
